# Roost — itch.io page copy

**Tagline:** Flap above the lava, unseat rival riders from above, and grab the eggs before they hatch.

## Description

Roost is a 1-bit aerial joust for the Panic Playdate. You ride a long-necked
heron over a lava pit; a flock of mounted raptors wants you in it. Every
collision is decided by height — whoever's lance rides higher wins the clash
and the loser bursts into a tumbling egg. Flap to climb, steer to line up your
attack, and always come in from *above*.

Unseat a rider and it drops an egg — snatch it for points before it hatches,
because a hatched egg comes back as a bird one tier tougher. Clear each wave to
face faster, smarter fliers (Fledgling, Harrier, Roc), bank a survival bonus
for a flawless wave, and watch the sky for a screeching pterodactyl that can
only be killed by a perfect head-on lance. All of the art is drawn
procedurally in code — no external image assets — with a crisp 1-bit look and a
little synth crunch.

## Features

- Flap-to-fly aerial jousting where **height wins the clash** — attack from
  above or get unseated
- Three tiers of rival riders — **Fledgling → Harrier → Roc** — that grow
  faster and hunt the high ground as you climb
- **Eggs that hatch** into tougher birds if you don't collect them in time,
  with a mid-air catch bonus and a per-wave scoring chain
- A screeching **pterodactyl** interloper that's invincible from every angle
  but one
- Lava floor that kills riders and rivals alike, plus horizontal screen wrap
  to escape a bird that's above you
- Escalating waves, a survival bonus for a no-death wave, extra lives, and a
  saved high score
- Fully **procedural 1-bit art** (no image files) and a synth sound set
- Play with **A** or use the **crank** to flap

## Controls

- **D-pad left / right** — steer
- **A** *or* **Crank** — flap
- **A** — start / play again

## Install

Download `Roost.pdx.zip` from Releases (or `dist/`), then sideload it at
https://play.date/account/sideload/ — or unzip it and open the `.pdx` in the
Playdate Simulator.
